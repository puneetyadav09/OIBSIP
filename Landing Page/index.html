Masala Dosa



Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian dish that has gained widespread popularity not only within India but also internationally. It is a delicious and savory crepe made from fermented rice and urad dal (black gram) batter, which is filled with a spiced potato mixture. Masala Dosa is a staple of South Indian cuisine and is enjoyed as a breakfast item, snack, or even a main course.
